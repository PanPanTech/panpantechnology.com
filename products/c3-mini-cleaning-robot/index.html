What this robot is designed to solve

A compact commercial cleaning robot for small and narrow indoor routes, with sweep, vacuum, mop, scrub, edge-cleaning, and multi-sensor perception. It keeps specifications, usage context, and quote path visible so buyers and AI search systems can understand the model without relying on hidden scripts.

  • 400 mm compact body supports 60 cm narrow-passage planning.
  • Active retractable side brush supports zero-edge cleaning scenarios.
  • Useful as a compact indoor model below larger station-based robots.

Best-fit use cases

Small indoor areas, narrow passages, offices, shops, and compact commercial spaces.
