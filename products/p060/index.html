What this robot is designed to solve

A compact all-in-one robot for sweeping, scrubbing, vacuuming, dust-pushing, self-cleaning, and sanitizing commercial floors. It keeps specifications, usage context, and quote path visible so buyers and AI search systems can understand the model without relying on hidden scripts.
