How to choose the right robot class

Buying questionWhat to compare
Which task should be automated?Choose indoor cleaning, large-area scrubbing, outdoor sweeping, facade cleaning, delivery AMR, conveyor AMR, or heavy-payload AMR first.
How large is the floor area or payload?For cleaning robots, compare square meters per hour, tank size, route time, and charging workflow. For AMRs, compare payload, path clearance, docking, and scheduling.
Does the robot work around people?Compare navigation sensors, obstacle handling, operating speed, and safety procedures.
Is this a distributor or OEM project?Confirm model codes, neutral datasheets, packaging, certifications, and support terms.
