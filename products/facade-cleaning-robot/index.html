What this robot is designed to solve

A high-rise facade and glass curtain wall cleaning robot for property teams seeking safer, repeatable exterior cleaning workflows. It keeps specifications, usage context, and quote path visible so buyers and AI search systems can understand the model without relying on hidden scripts.
